About
We are a small startup consulting firm of UC Berkeley students in San Diego, CA. Our team of Intuiterns has created a prototype for the new and improved app, Mint for Students. Through our SIC plan, we have: 1) Simplified the UI, 2) Improved the UX, and 3) Connected to other apps. We hope our product will inspire college students to save money and become financially responsible members of society, based off Intuit's core values of delivering awesome and giving back to the community.
